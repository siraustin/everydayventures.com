About Everyday Ventures

Everyday Ventures LLC is a product innovation studio based in Charlotte, North Carolina. Led by founder and inventor Luke Stuber, the studio builds products that are intuitive, joyful, and ready for the real world.

We collaborate with inventors, startups, and established brands, offering a full spectrum of services from concept to commercialization.

Why partners choose us

  • Hands-on experience launching consumer products
  • Human-centered design process rooted in empathy
  • Agile collaboration tailored to each founder's goals
  • Focus on manufacturability and market readiness
